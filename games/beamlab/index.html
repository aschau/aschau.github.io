How to Play

Route the laser beam from the source to all targets by placing mirrors on the grid.

▸ The arrow is the laser source. Circles are targets — hit them all to win.
/ Mirrors ( / and \ ) reflect the beam 90 degrees.
X Splitters split the beam — one path reflects, the other continues straight through.
■ Dark squares are walls — the laser stops when it hits one.
🔒 Gold pieces are fixed — they're already placed and can't be moved. The beam bounces off them like normal mirrors.
☛ Tap to place a piece. Tap again to cycle its type. Double-tap or right-click to remove.
★ Fewer pieces = better score. Beat par for bragging rights.
💎 Route the beam through the gem for a bonus collectible.
