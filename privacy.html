Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Overview

This website (raggedydoc.com) is a personal portfolio, game, and tool site operated by Andrew Steven Chau. This site does not use analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising. It does not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to any server.

Local Storage & Session Storage

This site uses your browser's localStorage and sessionStorage to save preferences and progress locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser. Examples include:

  • Game saves, puzzle progress, and usernames (Beamlab, Parsed)
  • Tool preferences such as room layouts (SnapLayout) and filter settings (Misery Index)
  • Portfolio features like visited-page tracking and achievement badges
  • Theme and display preferences
  • Whether the loading screen has been shown this session

You can clear this data at any time through your browser's settings (e.g. "Clear site data" for raggedydoc.com).

Third-Party Embeds

Some pages embed content from third-party services. When these embeds load, the third party may collect data (such as your IP address and cookies) according to their own privacy policies:

  • YouTube — video embeds (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Steam — store widget (Steam Privacy Policy)
  • X (Twitter) — tweet embeds (X Privacy Policy)
Google Fonts

Some pages load fonts from Google Fonts, which may transmit your IP address and browser user-agent to Google. See the Google Privacy Policy for details.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information such as your IP address in server logs. See the GitHub Privacy Statement for details.

Cookies

This site does not set any first-party cookies. However, third-party embeds (YouTube, Steam, X) may set their own cookies when their content loads.

Children's Privacy

This site does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted by this site, there is no data to manage in this regard.
