Date or Die

2014 — Made with PowerPoint. Yes, really.

Description

A dating simulator / visual novel built entirely in PowerPoint using hyperlinks and slide transitions. Click through the slides to make choices and navigate the story.

Fall madly in love... or die trying!

Role

Designer

Download & Play

Download the PowerPoint file and open it in presentation mode to play. Click the hyperlinks within the slides to make choices and navigate the story.
